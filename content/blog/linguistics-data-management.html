Introduction

These videos form part of a training course on Linguistic Data Management run by Nick Thieberger at the University of Melbourne on March 26th 2020. The course was sponsored by SCIP, the Research Unit for Indigenous Language, and the Pacific and Regional Archive for Digital Sources in Endangered Cultures (PARADISEC), and the ARC Centre of Excellence for the Dynamics of Language.

Software required

The software demonstrated in these videos include:

  • Elan and Simple Elan which can be downloaded from https://tla.mpi.nl/tools/tla-tools/elan/

  • FLEx (Windows only) which can be downloaded from https://software.sil.org/fieldworks/download/

  • Lameta which can be downloaded from https://github.com/onset/laMETA/releases
